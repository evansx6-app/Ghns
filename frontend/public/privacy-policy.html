Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 6, 2025
Last Updated: October 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Greatest Hits Non-Stop ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our radio streaming application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Voluntary Information: Any information you voluntarily provide through contact forms or feedback
  • User Preferences: Settings you choose within the app (volume levels, display preferences)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app (play/pause actions, time spent listening)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Network Information: IP address (for streaming functionality only)
  • Audio Playback Data: Current track information, listening session duration

1.3 Third-Party Data

  • Stream Metadata: Track titles, artist names, and album information from streaming services
  • Album Artwork: Images retrieved from music databases (MusicBrainz, Cover Art Archive)
  • Lyrics Data: Song lyrics from Lyrics.ovh API

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide radio streaming functionality
  • Content Display: To show current track information, lyrics, and album artwork
  • App Functionality: To enable features like visualizer, sleep timer, and track history
  • Performance Improvement: To monitor app performance and fix technical issues
  • User Experience: To remember your preferences and improve the app experience

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Services

We work with the following third-party services:

  • Stream Providers: MyRadioStream.com for radio streaming
  • Music Databases: MusicBrainz for track metadata and artwork
  • Lyrics Services: Lyrics.ovh for song lyrics
  • Image Services: Unsplash for fallback images

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

3.3 No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: App preferences and cache data are stored locally on your device
  • Temporary Data: Streaming data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • Security Measures: We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Cache Management: Album artwork and track information are cached for 24 hours to improve performance

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can request information about data we collect
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data by uninstalling the app
  • Correction: You can update your preferences within the app settings
  • Opt-out: You can stop using the app at any time

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to remove such information.

7. International Users

Our app may be used by people from different countries. By using our app, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different privacy laws than your country of residence.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing notice within the app if changes are significant

9. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as the contact form). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. Data Retention

  • Usage Data: Retained only during app session
  • Cache Data: Automatically cleared after 24 hours
  • Preferences: Stored until app uninstallation
  • No Long-term Storage: We do not maintain user accounts or long-term data storage

11. Cookie Policy

Our app may use local storage and cache mechanisms to improve performance. These are not traditional web cookies but serve similar functions for app functionality.
